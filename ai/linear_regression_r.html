Linear Regression in R¶

This notebook analyzes salary data with simple linear regression, visualizes the relationship between years of experience and salary, fits a predictive model, and summarizes model performance.

Read regression_data.csv and display the dataset.

plot(

Step 3: Fit a linear regression model¶

Fit a simple linear model predicting salary from years of experience using lm().

In [3]:
model <- lm(Salary ~ YearsExperience, data = data)

Step 5: Evaluate and summarize the model¶

Review the full model summary, R-squared, RMSE, and a sample prediction.

In [5]:
model_summary <- summary(model)
model_summary

r2 <- model_summary$r.squared
rmse <- sqrt(mean(residuals(model)^2))
predict_5yr <- predict(model, newdata = data.frame(YearsExperience = 5.0))

cat(sprintf("R-squared: %.6f\n", r2))
cat(sprintf("RMSE: %.4f\n", rmse))
cat(sprintf("Predicted salary at 5 years experience: %.2f\n", predict_5yr))
